Title: Restoring a cancelled reservation
Body: <p class="no-margin">If the Host or Guest cancels a reservation that has not yet been paid for, but the customer still wishes to come, there is a possibility to restore such a reservation.</p>
<p class="no-margin">To restore a canceled reservation, you need to:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Log in to your profile at www.alohacamp.com</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the upper right corner go to the menu and select <b>My Objects</b> and choose <b>Reservations</b>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the <i>Canceled</i> tab you will find all our canceled reservations. Select the reservation you want to restore and click <b>Edit Reservation.</b></p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the editing window we have the possibility to change the place, dates the number of Guests or the price.</p>
</li>

<p class="no-margin">6. After saving the changes, it will automatically extend the time for payment by <b>1 hour. </b></p>
<p class="no-margin">7. You can extend this time by going once again into Edit Reservation and add additional hours for payment.</p>
