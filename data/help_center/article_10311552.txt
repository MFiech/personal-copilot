Title: Discounts - Last Minute, First Minute offers and discounts for long stay
Body: <p class="no-margin">Introducing relevant promotions is a proven way to increase interest and attract new Guests. As a Host, you can take advantage of popular types of discounts such as Last Minute, First Minute and long stay discounts to highlight your property on the AlohaCamp website. Each of these types of promotions has the potential to attract different groups of Guests - from advance planners to spontaneous travelers to those looking for longer accommodations. </p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<h2 id="h_ee53f8a595">Last Minute Offers </h2>
<p class="no-margin">If you have a free date soon or one of your guests has cancelled at the last moment, you can set up a Last Minute offer.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Please note that this option is only available for properties with instant booking activated. Click <a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/8249916-instant-booking" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">here</a> to find out more.<br>​<br>​<b>Setting up Last Minute offers:</b></p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Log into your AlohaCamp account: <a href="https://alohacamp.com/en" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">https://alohacamp.com/en</a></p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Go to the <i>My Places</i> tab and then select <i>Discounts </i>section</p>
<p class="no-margin">3. Select <i>Add New</i>, in the window with information about the Last Minute offer.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307038380/a58cc0194050fee37bc0a4bb738b/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=d999dbd0e1c67073a87d22ed182171f79e3766d42e99e55de62ec17cfacddb3a&amp;req=dSMnEcl9lYJXWfMW1HO4zfCXilJMkJvmq9X1RsYLz%2FdPrq3u4EEjv5vJB1dQ%0AlMHY%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"><br>4. Select the dates for which the promotion should apply, set the value of the discount and then save the changes.</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307039767/bd4d72cf6974184fcd1a05452f61/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=aa15ebd57541a98e72fa553adf862c33e21795f6328e5e9f76db1630bf39f434&amp;req=dSMnEcl9lIZZXvMW1HO4zc%2FwnLKGAq418uIwpCp9hMi5854u7NiymmH8UqyH%0AV73g%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><br>Once saved, the offer will be valid in the discount panel in the Last Minute section. It can be removed at any time by clicking on the icon under the <i>Options</i>.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307040935/60650f6d39246ea0ad67b663cae6/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=7d9ec7024951855cf51301ba77b66913ba1bb9c3b2c76a1aee9d024c3a6f7af5&amp;req=dSMnEcl6nYhcXPMW1HO4zTvFZ1LQCDqWhp0Mrb41aImKNklkGwciFGrVzGXN%0A%2FGMA%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">Once the changes have been saved, your offer will appear on your property's homepage, allowing guests to quickly make a booking. In addition, information about the Last Minute offer will be displayed next to the name of your property, increasing its visibility.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307049727/2ecb399a81e4ad13f7734ab68d54/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=2f8413a9321a1048612687d90d687d70e7e1f3740fddba85746113a02d6b9e69&amp;req=dSMnEcl6lIZdXvMW1HO4zdbRwogzifQdvuj%2BS1HQUYCVwxFvWherX3cYkFQX%0AdAwR%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<h2 id="h_63857a19e1">First Minute offers </h2>
<p class="no-margin">If you want to attract guests who plan their trips in advance, adding a First Minute offer is the perfect solution. With this promotion, you can secure early bookings, allowing you to better manage your calendar and guests will be happy to take advantage of attractive prices that reward them for planning ahead.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>Important</b>: You can add the First Minute discount now, but please note that it will take effect and be visible to Guests from <b>01.01.2025</b>. </p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>Setting up a First Minute Offer:</b></p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Log into your AlohaCamp account: <a href="https://alohacamp.com/en" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">https://alohacamp.com/en</a></p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Go to <i>My properties</i> and then select <i>Discounts</i> section</p>
<p class="no-margin">3. Select <i>Add New</i>, in the window with information about First Minute Offers.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307881070/35d215e26f403f37f7b89e7797fa/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=a4de97af6b5f1b3404be17bd9a8566e90d6bce550a04ffb209a2e3dca559a825&amp;req=dSMnEcF2nIFYWfMW1HO4zc3%2B15%2BDLvtL6LUciwMVJO2eN%2BswdybgXsPSomLk%0AquCE%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>Then, in the window that appears: </b></p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Select the place for which you are adding the discount</p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Select the number of months for the advance booking - possible choices are 2,3,6 or 9 months.</p>
<p class="no-margin">3. Select the value of the discount - the minimum value is 10%.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Once the offer has been set up,<i> add the discount</i>.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307882096/ef84e798a357294d1028088a73d8/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=e4f2f363e01ab21b9e2205a9d5bebd3ac2ad7de88a118a942f5e2f84bdd1976a&amp;req=dSMnEcF2n4FWX%2FMW1HO4zQBXGeGN7XJvYiaKSHluD4hcfHxJyBobzYR5G0EF%0AlDO2%0A" width="223"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Then, the added discount will appear in the list in the same window. At this stage, you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Save the list</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Add more discounts if you have other places for which you want to set up a First Minute offer</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Edit or delete a discount you have added</p>
</li>
</ul><p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307883254/ce3ea659dcc735aaf5e734b0db90/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=485e4c2007cbc3ad8def07a975cfc105f7ae502130965fe3ff1df4d7508279e9&amp;req=dSMnEcF2noNaXfMW1HO4zSQSZelogleir1n50W7MsFnLdnTLwtVfyVvHhnIg%0AC6Mb%0A" width="640"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Once saved, the offer will be valid in the discount panel in the Last Minute section. It can be removed or edited at any time by clicking on the icon under the <i>Options</i>.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307884371/969f11d8c1e9300fa9322ab5c73d/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=4ae4d6d87caa4628ea994277407278488cfd38d0582d57171611173041cee253&amp;req=dSMnEcF2mYJYWPMW1HO4zYL5pGjeAq2XYJIiEZQDkgoJkmdK8zusEKIq4K6N%0AP9Sv%0A"></div><h2 id="h_c4e07d0988">
<br>Long stay discounts</h2>
<p class="no-margin">Longer bookings not only mean greater comfort for your guests, but also stable occupancy for your property. By offering discounts for long stays, you can attract people planning longer visits, allowing you to manage bookings more conveniently and benefiting both you and your guests.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>Important:</b> You can add a long stay discount now, but please note that it will take effect and be visible to Guests from <b>01.01.2025</b>. <br><br><b>Setting up a discount for a long stay:</b></p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Log in to your AlohaCamp account: <a href="https://alohacamp.com/en" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">https://alohacamp.com/en</a></p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Go to <i>My properties</i> and then select <i>Discounts</i> section</p>
<p class="no-margin">3. Select <i>Add New</i>, in the window with information about Long stay discounts<br><br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307885788/a8d36259967f61bd881754cd01e9/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=9f78fb520e62ef48e9a0dca9aafffd52f7026ab04f8e37b10ab1a25ca0def0c1&amp;req=dSMnEcF2mIZXUfMW1HO4za7PksIiBaGoI6iiBmnhAQmxm9vGaLJPGpnmIR7X%0A5k%2BK%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>In the new window that appears: </b></p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Select the place for which you are adding the discount</p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Select the length of stay - possible choices are over 4, 7, 14, 28 nights. </p>
<p class="no-margin">3. Select the value of the discount - the minimum value is 10%.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Once the offer has been set, <i>Add Discount </i></p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>

<p class="no-margin">Then, your discount will appear in the list. At this stage, you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Save the list</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Edit a discount you have added</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Add more discounts, for the same or different places you have. </p>
</li>
</ul><div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1307887673/63b0a1a07077466a7a4432107b43/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=71fc517f83506849363e85e3521812cb8e616d9e2bb813b7eda97d2434da6d88&amp;req=dSMnEcF2modYWvMW1HO4zRsl%2BcIfwzseKT90LvAiWeZfqQP9%2FKcoU%2BDKc1J7%0AbR2k%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Once saved, the offer will be valid in the discount panel in the Last Minute section. It can be removed or edited at any time by clicking on the icon under the <i>Options</i>.</p>
