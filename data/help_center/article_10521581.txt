Title: Outdoors Club
Body: <p class="no-margin">Outdoors Club is a loyalty program designed for Guests using the AlohaCamp platform, allowing them to access exclusive discounts and special offers available only within the club. For you, as a Host, this means greater visibility for your property and the ability to attract Guests looking for attractive deals who are more likely to book places offering club benefits.<br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">You can find more information about Outdoors Club in a dedicated tab <a href="https://alohacamp.com/en/outdoors-club" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">Outdoors Club</a>, which is also visible in the top menu next to the Host panel.<br>​</p>

<h3 id="h_1d677404e2"><b>How to join Outdoors Club as a Host?</b></h3>
<p class="no-margin">If you are not yet a member of the club, when you enter the Outdoors Club tab, you will find two "I want to join" buttons. The first one is at the top of the page, and the second is in a floating panel on the bottom bar. The system will then ask you to accept the terms and conditions as well as consent to receive marketing content. Both options are mandatory to join the club.<br>​<br>​</p>

<h3 id="h_c17ef84573"><b>Outdoors Club and discounts</b></h3>
<p class="no-margin">All discounts that a Host adds to their property are visible only to Guests who have joined Outdoors Club. Other users cannot take advantage of them, so if they decide to book a stay at your property, the standard price without discounts will apply. You can read more about discounts <b><a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/10311552-discounts-last-minute-first-minute-offers-and-discounts-for-long-stay" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">[here]</a></b>.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>

<h3 id="h_2d9d2fbd8d"><b>Why is it worth offering discounts in Outdoors Club?</b></h3>
<p class="no-margin">Adding attractive promotions, such as discounts, makes your place more popular, leading to more bookings. Discounts also allow participation in periodic promotional campaigns, further increasing Guest interest in your property.</p>
<p class="no-margin">Seasonal offers, such as the January cashback, encourage Guests to book more readily. By introducing discounts, your properties stand out from the competition, attracting both returning Guests and new ones looking for appealing, personalized deals.</p>
