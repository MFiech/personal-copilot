Title: Changing and adding accounts for payouts
Body: <p class="no-margin">You can change the account number to which payments for your bookings come, add another account, or check which one is currently linked to your Stripe account.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">To do so, you need to:</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Log in to Stripe: https://dashboard.stripe.com/login</p>
<p class="no-margin">If you need help, read our article: <b><a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/8247355-sign-in-to-stripe-and-dashboard" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">Sign in to Stripe and Dashboard</a></b></p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Go to your account settings in the upper right corner:</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858034462/7156a7d57292822e5d0e6c7c/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=3a2ef72e83368f82256be264cf63932595cdca3e0f31bec0e7fe5aa8c9acd6e4&amp;req=fCUvFsp6mYddFb4f3HP0gCScWFuHpMwJ7%2FFJJTUWZLFOc9NSIsMtoTGWVfCI%0AAig%3D%0A" width="206"></div><p class="no-margin">3.       Go to the <b>Payout Details</b> tab:</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858037237/867289f2c8d7ed26d6fbd875/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=643a032580736e0280d3cd3895bfed36c286e9f54361e42f93b6998acdc25da3&amp;req=fCUvFsp5n4JYFb4f3HP0gHObKm5I1%2BFC7mWYFD9xhbjZQe7Vx6YaKmSOUc28%0AIfY%3D%0A"></div><p class="no-margin">The preview will show the current account for payments:</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858040364/b262ff5c63aabaab8bed64f3/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=d2b5e95f6ba78758e5091f3a18d332d4d8760dc84a0b0b30e7da0679cad7d059&amp;req=fCUvFs1%2BnodbFb4f3HP0gC8PtbCWmLqYVP%2Fcr23UkRoP3yTicybP9g6NP4k6%0ABPU%3D%0A" width="456"></div><p class="no-margin">If you want to change only the account number, click the edit symbol (pencil), then enter the new IBAN and save the changes. If you are also changing the country of the bank account, first select it from the list and then enter the other data you will be asked for.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858041072/98ba7bd6a0e3f29907e0f80d/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=3b9f98844afa1be80abc4f1105167bc49c380fdd97f9009f73b15542049e7610&amp;req=fCUvFs1%2FnYZdFb4f3HP0gHtxvwycpReUbL606EoPTdgD9WtjYpPnqlZDqQNW%0AYuU%3D%0A" width="403"></div><p class="no-margin">If you want to add another account for payments, click "<b>Add account</b>" and then complete all required blanks and save changes.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858048384/0179d93e0f99f34bfc55fd8d/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=c599ed934bbfbb99138ce700112c4d1b03bdd1e266ee8fdfeb98bf9cfad7af08&amp;req=fCUvFs12nolbFb4f3HP0gCyh3k%2BzXPyHXxLkYIaLiIaSTHp2N7W39mb2lsps%0AtG0%3D%0A" width="364"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858051739/52cc17c3b0334f63509d56e5/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=b72949b9fc9bea06ff46c77440ede616d056c4dfbef57cdae32273e0c78dbe99&amp;req=fCUvFsx%2FmoJWFb4f3HP0gGETlUXPmNTs%2B%2B7wzZ8rehBxgSk6ssu0pHFQ0K29%0ASeA%3D%0A" width="400"></div><p class="no-margin">To change the default account, click "<b>Change Default Currency</b>", then select the appropriate one and confirm with "<b>Submit</b>."</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/858067524/6d594f23b5b7e21fced8ad6a/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=f9b80ad4222527386edde41d9f6a3a260a70f5726a87316a8a9f917be339b1b0&amp;req=fCUvFs95mINbFb4f3HP0gHNplNEkfWJP2t006QucJsQRnwz2WFaV%2FstDrh%2F5%0AdkM%3D%0A" width="300"></div><p class="no-margin">To delete any of the accounts, you need to go to edit the account and select the "<b>Remove Account</b>" option. This is only possible if you have several accounts added to your account.</p>
