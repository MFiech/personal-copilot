Title: Last Minute offers
Body: <p class="no-margin">If you have an available date soon or some of your guests canceled their stay at the last moment, it is possible to set a Last Minute offer.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Remember that this option is available only for the properties with activated instant reservations. Click <a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/8249916-instant-booking" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">here</a> to find out more about it.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">To set up such an offer: </p>
<p class="no-margin">1. Log in to your AlohaCamp account: <a href="https://alohacamp.com/en" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">https://alohacamp.com/en</a></p>
<p class="no-margin">2. Go to the <b>Types of accommodation, prices</b> tab.</p>
<p class="no-margin">3. At the very bottom of the page you will find the option "<b>Add Last Minute Offer</b>".</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>

Select the dates for which you want the promotion to apply, specify the percentage discount you want to give and save the changes.</p>
