Title: Set prices
Body: <p class="no-margin">Remember to keep the prices updated at all times. Thanks to this, Guests will<br>always see the correct price and you will not have to worry about the value of<br>the reservation being miscalculated.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>How to set prices?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Log in to <a href="http://www.alohacamp.com/" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">www.alohacamp.com</a><br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the main menu select <b>My places</b>.<br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Choose the place where you want to update the rates and go to <b>Types of accommodation, prices.</b><br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Select the place where you want to edit the pricing and click on <b>Edit prices</b>.<br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Set your base price per night.</p>
<ul><li>
<p class="no-margin">You can choose whether you want to receive payments in PLN or EUR.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369631288/3542ae2eeaa90da3f792606007be/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=b65915a072eba74265cf76de1866ddaae2f0b27fd179814fd58c93869f0bd6a3&amp;req=dSMhH899nINXUfMW1HO4zeXCoRbwWOwRwfT%2BcX08oaPTw38zFRsv76pZy4Hq%0A0Z9Q%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Seasonal Pricing – If your prices vary at certain times of the year, you can set up Seasons. To do this, click <b>Add New</b>.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369632004/85e40f8dc5b790ef350a58818908/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=66459106ceb5f3a981d6a1ceb578dd500b131faa3e52b9373fa2418b7159caf5&amp;req=dSMhH899n4FfXfMW1HO4ze7h1q5EHu5UpqHBRkCrcTAYV8g%2BIGNo1hRhmw7b%0AOuk4%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Specify the date range for the seasonal pricing, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">The price for weekdays and weekends.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">The minimum number of nights required for booking during that period.<br>​</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369633150/79475b23a4b24f66d8edc012439d/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=071db439047a1ba7e2d34c1cbe207cb9d2ddcc5ee1b609650fbcb0245866295d&amp;req=dSMhH899noBaWfMW1HO4zbjyeukceRtJkfgGyngQpGXRiYXgPLRMM0QbcTZY%0ACHYA%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>Important:</b> Seasonal settings do not automatically transfer to the next year. For example, if you set seasonal pricing for Summer 2025, it will not be copied to 2026.<br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Set up <b>additional guest fees</b> if applicable.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">If you charge an extra fee for additional guests, move the slider to the right.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Define from which guest the additional charge applies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Enter the amount of the extra fee.<br><br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369633727/d4ea1592980ad68645600a56c4bc/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=a1bb999c712dc84a2a760f9c8bb7305a1cf5f2a7c7acadcabae70c1709633d91&amp;req=dSMhH899noZdXvMW1HO4zTkcWzp94zNIXisGAyH1lTzAhduLj4NSg4NOFBoG%0AYtL2%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin">​<br>​</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Enable <b>advances</b> if you want to allow partial payments.</p>
<ul><li>
<p class="no-margin">Specify what percentage of the reservation amount should be collected through AlohaCamp.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369637416/77de009887ef7f490551d4ca1caf/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=79f4975a80ccf10eba9545dd78f6af1cdcf8748430c166531c37ad0b7c46353d&amp;req=dSMhH899moVeX%2FMW1HO4zZohhT6ZJhcnZfB50GvgjpyIcNT13HVmi9%2BoQ5Ua%0AsMjr%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​<br><b>Important!</b> When making a reservation, the Guest will have two payment options – full payment or deposit.<br><br>You can read more about <a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/8249966-prepayments" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">advances</a> here.<br>​</p>
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Similarly to setting up seasons, you can also add <b>special discounts</b>, which allow for even better price management and offering attractive deals that will attract more Guests. All details regarding discount settings and instructions can be found at <a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/10311552-discounts-last-minute-first-minute-offers-and-discounts-for-long-stay" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">this link</a>.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369639023/b884207d49090e596dd4e1e241dd/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=142a7ee5da51c9ab29bfbd5573657fbe37591af80b635db8a6b7ad8a2698a278&amp;req=dSMhH899lIFdWvMW1HO4zSfWsAL7df3aQDz8y0aS9%2FeEG5OxaZmAFrLWi5%2FK%0A7%2BNU%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the Check-in Preferences section, we provide tools that make managing your property easier. You can specify the minimum number of nights a Guest can book. (According to our statistics, Guests most often choose reservations lasting 2 nights).<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369639469/ef648a86cc63344a8f4d2c273bcb/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=ce35989152b98bc39cbf250f12509ce5ebb96e506ce4c54f5826411a874ecdc2&amp;req=dSMhH899lIVZUPMW1HO4zZOD2fg3RYpZWunTpCu7RHNvhykw%2BAJctzzRGpVU%0ASRAU%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">The Instant Booking feature helps save time – with it, you don’t have to respond to every request, and your calendar will only be blocked for 1 hour.<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369639807/037a6d9854029b097276a0f2e4e8/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=13d418d2a3970d03839bd63e52d06edca5bcef58b4df01d480de4efe07cc78e7&amp;req=dSMhH899lIlfXvMW1HO4zacO1ll%2FmdPclDv%2BU4dSRO20n3OSE490DTV2eykA%0AjcBH%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>Good to know:</b> Enabling Instant Booking allows you to avoid an additional 2% commission, which means greater savings for you. If you want more control over your calendar, you can easily adjust settings such as the <b>Pre-arrival booking minimum time</b> or <b>Calendar auto-blocking</b>. This way, you can plan everything according to your needs and avoid unexpected last-minute reservations!<br>​</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369641389/920712393d330015fc10fa13cb36/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=9446ffa4d4f585dc0d163a81ac9c3295ce986803b98ee982261656d28f4e2ea6&amp;req=dSMhH896nIJXUPMW1HO4zZFVCrkAOP9IwaWDXOyHhuojxh%2Bu%2BUFby21%2FY9Iz%0AfJGM%0A"></div>
<p class="no-margin"><br>​</p>
</li>
</ul><ul><li>
<p class="no-margin">If the minimum length of stay at your property is at least 2 nights, but you don’t want gaps in your calendar, it is worth using the <b>Intelligent Gap Unlocking</b> function, which increases the chances of fully booking available reservation dates.<br>​</p>
</li></ul><div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/xa8cb3gu/1369643342/f536ff486fe7d0bf1fb3fe5c8bf9/image.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=4a22cce31d45627fd01e516d957059171ff270748192552d16ac6d97cd6cac58&amp;req=dSMhH896noJbW%2FMW1HO4zeFWzywnIUlkeRLas56IRzVRkqUfuEeGf51I72X9%0ANwT4%0A"></div><p class="no-margin"><b>How does Intelligent Gap Unlocking work?</b><br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">If gaps appear in your calendar and the minimum length of stay prevents them from being booked, the system will automatically adjust the minimum number of nights required during that period to make the booking possible.<br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">The minimum number of nights will never be lowered below 2 nights unless you have enabled the option: "<b>Allow 1-night reservations if the minimum stay is 2 nights (including seasons).</b>" In this case, the system will allow 1-night bookings.<br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">The tool considers all pricing settings, seasons, and advance booking restrictions that you have configured.</p>
<p class="no-margin"><b>​</b><br><br><b>Example of how it works:</b><br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">If you set the minimum stay to 3 nights, but a 2-night gap appears in your calendar, our algorithm will automatically allow a 2-night booking during that period.<br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin">Thanks to this feature, you can increase the number of reservations without additional effort while maintaining full control over your settings.<br>​</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">The final step in configuration is check-in settings.</p>
<p class="no-margin">Define which days you consider weekends so that we can correctly calculate Guest stays.<br>​</p>

<p class="no-margin">​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">If there are specific days on which Guests can check in, mark them in the settings. This will ensure greater transparency and make reservation management easier for both you and your Guests.<br>​​</p>
