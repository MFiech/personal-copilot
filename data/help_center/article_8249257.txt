Title: Smart unblocking of calendar gaps
Body: <p class="no-margin">If there are gaps in your calendar and the minimum number of nights you set will not allow clients to book them, the system will automatically modify the minimum number of nights in a given period so that the booking can take place. This functionality is activated by default for all Hosts, but you can turn it off anytime.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">How to activate or deactivate <b>smart unblocking of calendar gaps</b>?</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Log in to your profile at <a href="http://www.alohacamp.com" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">www.alohacamp.com</a></p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the top menu, select <b>My Places</b>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Then, in the property where you want to make the change, go to <b>Types of accommodation, prices.</b></p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">You will find the option to tick/un-tick <b>Smart Unblocking of Calendar Gaps</b>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Activate or deactivate the option and <b>save the changes</b> via the save button at the bottom of the page and wait for the green-framed confirmation to appear in the upper left corner.</p>
</li>
</ol><div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/805533704/b9e315839d2875e9bd964ab4/d3da8d32-f884-42b7-be42-bbc7a34b863a?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=39c42715e0920ea9c32e70d00d6e270e6f967042f0f5acea1b6417039cf0d9c1&amp;req=fCAiE8p9moFbFb4f3HP0gJwM6e6Huh6oYL0njuPmbhh5mubCJGHGH8IPSaVj%0A6Vk%3D%0A"></div><p class="no-margin">Remember that the minimum gaps detected by the system will never be less than 2 nights, except for properties with a minimum stay of 2 nights â€“ in such case the modification will allow one-day gaps to be booked.</p>
