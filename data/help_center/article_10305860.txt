Title: Guest Reviews – How to Manage Property Ratings?
Body: <h3 id="h_f6711b2c5b" class="intercom-align-justify"><b>What are Guest Reviews?</b></h3>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"><br>Guest reviews are a vital part of building trust and reputation for any property. They provide valuable feedback for future Guests looking for a place that meets their expectations, while also helping hosts better understand Guest needs and improve their services. This, in turn, leads to increased bookings.</p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"></p>

<h3 id="h_aa3c5b2737" class="intercom-align-justify">
<b>What does a review consist of?</b><br>
</h3>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin">Each review includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin">Star rating from 1 to 5</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin">Guest's comment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin">Host's reply<br>​ </p>
</li>
</ul><h3 id="h_6afaf69e83" class="intercom-align-justify"><b>How can Guests leave a review?</b></h3>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"><br>After the guest's stay, an email with a link to leave a review is sent. The first email arrives 24 hours post-checkout, with 3-4 reminder emails sent within the next month. Reviews can also be submitted directly from the reservation window.</p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"></p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"><b>Important! Only guests who have made a reservation and completed their stay at your property can leave a review.</b><br>​</p>

<h3 id="h_d1c31de991" class="intercom-align-justify"><b>Can a review be removed?</b></h3>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"><br>Sometimes you may receive a negative review. Reviews cannot be removed as this ensures transparency and builds trust with future Guests. Every user has the right to share a subjective opinion based on their experience at your property.</p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"></p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin">If you disagree with a review or find it unfair, we recommend reaching out to the Guest to clarify the situation. Upon their request, we will edit the review.</p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin"></p>
<p class="intercom-align-justify no-margin">If a review contains false information or was left by a Guest who did not stay at your property, please contact us via email at <a target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">hello@alohacamp.com</a> for verification and resolution.</p>
