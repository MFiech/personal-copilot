Title: Add reservations manually and manage the AlohaCamp calendar
Body: <p class="no-margin">Keeping the calendar updated is essential for any Host. This builds trust with Guests, speeds up their decision-making process, and allows AlohaCamp to effectively promote properties on our social media. We need to avoid situations where we offer Guests dates that are already taken, which is why keeping an updated calendar is so important.</p>
<p class="no-margin">If you save reservations in a channel manager or another reservation platform, such as Booking.com or Airbnb, we recommend that you <b>syncronize the calendar.</b></p>
<p class="no-margin">How to do it? We explain it <a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/8249295-how-to-synchronize-alohacamp-s-calendar-with-other-booking-platforms" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">here</a>.</p>
<p class="no-margin">In the case that you do not have the possibility to synchronize your calendar, it is necessary that you add all the reservations manually.</p>
<p class="no-margin">How to add an external reservation manually?</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Log in to <a href="http://www.alohacamp.com/" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">www.alohacamp.com</a></p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the main menu select <b>My places.</b> </p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Choose your place in which you want to synchronize the calendar and go to C<b>alendar</b>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Click on the <b>+Add</b> reservation button. </p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/805581212/07933019dd1f15e1fa9a10e4/Zrzut+ekranu+2023-08-11+o+2.46.21+PM.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=451cf9f35a4f62784d5557f8c1cdcccb02c86d88a9cc6f91941819bd72e060d9&amp;req=fCAiE8F%2Fn4BdFb4f3HP0gGpGvNK13sjvnue0JfjWc5ZIR13Fpv4IqGQBtQuL%0Abmc%3D%0A" width="200"></div>
</li>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">In the newly opened window, you will be able to select dates, slot (if you have several), guest's name, contact phone number and comments on the reservation. </p>
<p class="no-margin">Important: you can mark<b> all the slots </b>at once if you want to block the calendar or introduce the same reservations for all.</p>
<div class="intercom-container"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/805581746/55624c7469dce5c4ecab31a5/Zrzut+ekranu+2023-08-11+o+2.46.33+PM.png?expires=1740333600&amp;signature=a33cf496c10d6b7362d055b7c64b24e7995e8024e026adfecd55a1e40272ffc6&amp;req=fCAiE8F%2FmoVZFb4f3HP0gDU91vCO2GDXdZFlOznSSVR5Kv92h0aTwVr0y%2Bcf%0ARXw%3D%0A" width="300"></div>
</li>
</ol><p class="no-margin">6. After saving the changes in the lower right corner, you can find it in your calendar. If you have a synchronization done, our system will send information about the new booking to your external calendars and block the dates. A preview is displayed by clicking on the booking bar, where you can edit it again.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>

<p class="no-margin">It is possible to edit the dates, your guest's first name, last name, phone number, and comment. However, it is not possible to edit the place or slot. To make changes to these, you will need to delete the reservation and create a new one with the correct place or slot.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">If your calendar is synchronized, we recommend that you activate<b> instant reservations</b> at your facility. With this you won't have to confirm every reservation that enters and save you and your Guests time. Instant reservations speed up the decision-making process for Guests, resulting in more bookings. To read more about it and to learn how to activate this feature, <a href="https://help.alohacamp.com/en/articles/8249916-instant-booking" target="_blank" class="intercom-content-link">click here</a>.</p>
